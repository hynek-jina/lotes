# Privacy Policy for Lotes

## Introduction

Welcome to Lotes. This privacy policy outlines our practices concerning the handling of your personal data when you use our app.

## Our Commitment to Privacy

At Lotes, we are dedicated to protecting your privacy. Our app is designed to assist you in creating Bitcoin Lightning Network invoices without compromising your personal information.

## Information We Do Not Collect

- **Personal Information:** We do not collect any personal information including, but not limited to, your name, email address, or physical address.
- **Usage Data:** We do not track or store any information related to how you use our app.
- **Device Information:** No information about your device, such as the IP address, device type, or operating system, is collected or stored.
- **Financial Information:** We do not collect any financial information, including Bitcoin wallet addresses or transaction history.

## How We Protect Your Information

Since our app does not collect any personal data, there is no risk of your personal information being exposed or misused.
